Fried Rice!

This recipe is amazing for a fullfilling meal that you can eat alone or share with others. Its relatively easy to make and quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of steamed rice
  • your choice of chicken, pork or shrimp, cut into pieces
  • soy sauce
  • peas, carrots or both
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. First cook the selected choice meat on medium heat, add soy sauce then put to the side
  2. Then add the vegetables of your choice, if frozen cook until soft
  3. Add the eggs to the side, cook until scrambled and mix
  4. Finally add all of the ingredients together in the pan, and cook until the meat is warm again
  5. Serve on bowls after mixing and enjoy
