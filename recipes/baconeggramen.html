Bacon egg ramen!

!

This is the best ramen youve ever had, mixing ramen with your favorite breakfast items makes it amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ramen packet
  • Your choice of bacon, sliced into bits
  • soy sauce(optional)
  • cayenne pepper
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon on medium heat until its crisp to your liking
  2. Cook ramen until soft, add the ramen flavor packet and soy sauce if youd like
  3. Then cook the egg to scramble or poached
  4. Add the bacon and egg to a bowl with the noodles, add the noodle broth
  5. Then enjoy!
