Bacon Pancakes!

This breakfast recipe is great for yourself or to share with friends. Its relatively simple and very flexible.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of pancake batter
  • Bacon sliced into halves
  • Measuring cup, and nonstick pan

Steps

  1. First cook the bacon until crispy
  2. once the bacon is finished, pour the pancake batter onto the bacon, letting the batter envelope the bacon
  3. Flip the pancake when enough bubbles reach the surface
  4. Serve on a plate with your choice of syrup or butter and enjoy!
